The `<MeetingContainer />` component.

This component displays meeting details and action buttons supplied by the consumer

Meeting tags, such as recordings and messages can also be shown.

The two schedule info fields are intended to show date, start and end times, whether the meeting is in progress, and the duration - depending on the meeting state. A divider dot renders when the second field is used.

Avatar and Action buttons, supplied by the consumer adjust position based on the whether tags are supplied.

Arrays of both action buttons and tags are expected. 

The spaceLink should be supplied as a 'ButtonHyperlink'

Height = 'fit-content' overrides supplied style prop.
